TapID: The Tap that Connects

Empowering Students with Smart ID Cards

Project Idea: TapID by Coding Crusaders

TapID is a revolutionary project aimed at transforming the way students interact with their school environment.

Innovation

TapID aims to introduce smart ID cards to schools, leveraging NFC technology to provide a seamless and secure experience.

Convenience

TapID streamlines school operations by eliminating the need for cash transactions and providing access to essential information.

Efficiency

TapID enhances communication and data management by offering a dedicated web portal and Android app for parents.

Smart ID Cards For Students

TapID's smart ID cards are designed to be user-friendly and highly secure.

1

NFC Enabled

The cards utilize Near-Field Communication (NFC) technology, allowing for contactless interactions.

2

Secure Data Storage

Sensitive student information is securely encrypted and stored on the cards, preventing unauthorized access.

3

Durable and Reliable

The cards are made from high-quality materials that can withstand everyday wear and tear, ensuring longevity.

4

Personaliztion Options

TapID allows for customization with student photos, names, and other relevant details.
